♦ about me ♦


I’m studying molecular bioengineering at the BIOTEC facility of Technical University of Dresden, Germany.
Previously, I studied information and communication technology from DAIICT, India.
My areas of interest are design and self-assembly of DNA nanostructutres,
and their functionalization to organic molecules and inorganic particles.
I also enjoy programming software and web design.

My projects

Nanorover: Magnetic bead based system for drug transport & delivery

A three-part system comprised of a tractable magnetic swimmer, a DNA origami linker, and a functionalized liposome-peptide conjugate system. Nanorover was a 'gold award' winning project in BIOMOD 2016.
♦ liposome synthesis and encapsulation ♦ peptide mediated bursting ♦ DNA origami ♦ website design ♦

3DNA: A Tool for DNA Sculpting

An open source software tool for designing 3D nanostructures based on single-stranded DNA bricks. 3DNA offers the ability to sculpt nano sized structures, while generating random and unique DNA sequences for a one-pot assembly of the designed structures.
♦ software design ♦ DNA sequence design ♦ Java programming ♦ Java 3D ♦ video and web design ♦

DNA Pen: A Tool for Drawing on a Molecular Canvas

Based on the single-stranded DNA tiles, 'DNA Pen' is an open source software for designing 2D shapes at nanoscale, while generating DNA sequences on the go.
♦ software design ♦ DNA sequence design ♦ Java programming ♦ image detection ♦ video and web design ♦

e-PATHSHAALA: An online education portal

An online education platform assisting school students and teachers to interact, solve questions and engage in discussions.
♦ website design ♦ front end development ♦ software requirement study (SRS) ♦ database management ♦
